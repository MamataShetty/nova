Welcome & insurance collection

Get a head start on eligibility

Klara Assistant prompts patients to conveniently take a picture of their insurance card before their appointment. That means your team can kick off the verification process well before the patient sets foot in the office.

Appointment reminders

Reduce no-shows and late cancellations

Klara Assistant reminds patients about their upcoming appointments with customizable messages that communicate relevant details in a personal tone. Patient information automatically syncs on an hourly basis to ensure the right message goes to the right patient at the right time.

Instructions

Make every visit a success by helping patients prepare

Patients are busy and won’t always remember what they’re supposed to do before an upcoming procedure or visit. Klara Assistant sends timely texts letting patients know what to expect and how to prepare, including attachments and links that help get your message across.

No-show engagement

Get patients back on the calendar without the back-and-forth

Klara Assistant prevents patients from falling through the cracks by sending no-shows a message with a request to book a new appointment right from their phones (no login or password required). Integration with your EHR provides the patient with real-time information on your practice's availability.

Feedback & reviews

Show patients why your practice is the right choice

The best way to strengthen your reputation to stand out among the competition? Listen to your patients. Klara Assistant messages patients after their appointments with a quick satisfaction survey so you can get in front of any issues right away. Customize responses by score and drive patients to write online reviews.
