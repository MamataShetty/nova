Give patients easy access to your practice

We’ve made telemedicine simple and easy for you. Create your personal room and start practicing telemedicine today.

Invite a patient for their visit to your Nova URL

For a patient to enter your waiting room ahead of a call, they simply need to enter your personalized URL into their Web browser. You can share a link to your waiting room in many different ways, including several useful ways right from your dashboard. You can invite via calendar, SMS, email or by website badge/

Waiting room personalization

After your patient checks in to your room, they will be placed in your waiting room that can be personalized. In the waiting room, patients can view text and images, watch videos, send and receive a chat message.

Patients enter a queue

We designed the Patient Queue to give you more information on anyone in your waiting room that you might like to know before starting a call, such as: how long they have been waiting, their location, the strength of their wifi connection, which browser they're using, if they've given the browser access to their devices camera/microphone, What type of device and Operating System they're using.

Bring multiple parties to the video conversation

You can hold sessions with up to five participants in different locations. This is a great feature to have if you have a couple in different locations or need another specialist on the call.

Share your screen with your patient

Screenshare is an easy way to show your patient a website, test results, xray, or a document from your computer. Only microphone audio can be streamed during screenshare.

Take a picture of your patient

Photo capture is a simple and easy way to document a symptom, record ID or insurance card information, or record evidence of your appointment for insurance.

Send/Receive files from/to your patient

File transfer is a HIPAA compliant way to send files between you and your patient. This is a better alternative to emails as the data you send might contain PHI.

Collect payments from patients

The payments feature lets you accept credit card payments while on a call or from the waiting room. Once you link up your Stripe account (or equivalent in different countires), session payments and copays become way easier.

Record the video call with you patient

Entire telemedicine video consultation can be recorded and stored record purpose. These recorded files comply to HIPAA, PHI compliance and stored securely.

Chat with patients in the waiting room

Sometimes the patients have to wait in the waiting room for some periods of time. Doctor can send quick chat messages to the patients or send some reading materials/videos so the patient is bit engaged.

Get digital signatures with teleconsent

With teleconsent, doctors can get signatures for your documents from patients before the telemedicine call. Doctors can pick the right teleconsent templates from the repository.
